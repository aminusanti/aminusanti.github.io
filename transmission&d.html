  • Feasibility studies
  • Survey, development of line route maps and longitudinal profiles
  • Design of new lines, rehabilitation works and transmission line voltage or rating upgrading using PLS CADD
  • Preparation of inquiry folder, application for permits
  • Calculation of electromagnetic fields in accordance with the decree for protection against non-ionised radiation and corona effects
  • Investigation of environmental impacts
  • 3D-Visualisation of overhead transmission lines and substations
